YouTube is a platform where people can share their videos online and, it is one of the most popular platform and the second largest search engine behind Google Search.

YouTube is for everybody and its user base ranges from young to old, although is more popular among the younger people.

Any company can use YouTube for self-promotion.

It’s not about the business itself, but more about the marketing campaign you want to do. The probability your target audience is an YouTube user is extremely high and, learning about their behavior using it will be crucial for a successful self-promotion.

Pros Cons
It is simple and easy to share a video on it. Annoying advertising.
It is free. Poor customization and usability.
The videos can have any size or length. Easy to lose prospect to competition because of the content related proximity.
It is widely known and accepted. Negative reviews can damage a business reputation (dislikes and negative comments).
It can be linked to your website. Videos with same tags aren’t always content related.
Better SEO rankings. Lack of control over the content where your ads will appear
Viral effect. The skip button. It’s hard to hook an audience in five seconds or less.
Faster response from the public (comments and likes).

Some tricks and tips can help you to promote your company on Youtube. See bellow:

  • Monitor comments and respond quickly when appropriate.
  • Keep your videos short to maintain interest.
  • Include CTA (call to action) – “check our website, leave a comment, like this video, etc”.
  • Promote your customers with testimonials or videos of them using your product or services.
  • Encourage video sharing.
  • Improve the search engine optimization using the right tags and keywords in the video description.
  • Reinforce the brand – ex: put the logo in the beginning and end of the video.
  • Customize you channel – make it be related to your website (colors, logo, links).
  • Choose wisely with who your brand will be related.
